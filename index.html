    inter activity

    This website aims to optimize interactivity with responsiveness, using efficient fog keyframes to play with the position of the mouse and obscure elements on screen. By changing the size of elements on hover, the fog can be pushed down while revealing additional content. The theme is the development process as a mountain, and the mind as a universe. The highlighted development process exists as one planet in that universe, which can be explored further by scrolling down through the fog, or abandoned by choosing to explore the stars beyond.
