    inter activity

    This website aims to optimize interactivity with responsiveness, using efficient fog keyframes to play with the position of the mouse and obscure elements on screen. By changing the size of elements on hover, the fog can be pushed down while revealing additional content. The theme is the development process as a mountain, and the mind as a universe. The highlighted development process exists as one planet in that universe, which can be explored further by scrolling down through the fog, or abandoned by choosing to explore the stars beyond.

    Design

    Leveraging cognitive systems research to establish robust frameworks before entering the testing phase, ensuring our design decisions are informed by established principles of human behavior and interaction.

    "Everybody has a plan until they get punched in the mouth."

    Our approach uses research-driven documentation to guide development from the beginning, rather than discovering established behavioral patterns after testing.

    Production

    a series of incomplete steps, some further than others. Building a big project out of many little ones, bouncing between the side projects and the main project, climbing higher and higher until the two towers convene. "hard to see the woods through the trees"

    Our methodology revolves around planning large projects by dividing them into immediate and future tasks, connected by dependencies. Current tasks are organized along thematic axes, creating two towers of projects: creative and technical. The production process alternates between these towers to prevent burnout and maintain momentum, with teams working in logical succession.
