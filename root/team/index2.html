Our Team

  • The Mystic

    The Mystic sits at the helm of the boat, meditating and bringing luck to the crew.

  • The Cook

    The Cook is either in the kitchen preparing meals or out fishing for fresh ingredients.

  • The Valet

    The Valet stands at the helm, controlling the wheel and ensuring smooth sailing.

  • The Salesman

    The Salesman lounges around or assists the Cook in trading with locals.

  • The Tester

    The Tester explores the boat, helping with the sails, reporting repairs, and suggesting improvements.

  • The Designer

    The Designer is often in the library or looking out over the ocean with a spyglass.

  • The Builder

    The Builder helps with constructing and repairing parts of the boat.

  • The Warrior

    The Warrior stands ready to protect the crew, either behind the captain or at the cannons.
